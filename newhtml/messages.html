Staking

Here you can stake XRT transferred from Ethereum to Parachain in Exodus and get rewards.

Important tips:

  • Unbonding takes approx. 50 000 blocks
  • Once you have bonded any amount, you can't add to your stake more with chosen account. We are working on it and in the future this option should be available.
  • Crowdloan contributors will get increased reward rate.

Rewards Distribution
= 40Wn * XRT / block

Increased Rewards Distribution
= 200Wn * XRT / block
